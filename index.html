Our story

A place where good food finds great company

Perched on the 8th floor of Next Gen Avenue, High Garden was built for those who believe dinner is never just about the food. The rooftop offers a panoramic slice of Pune — the hum of SB Road below, and open sky above.

From Italian pizza to biryani, craft cocktails to mocktails — we bring the world's flavours to one rooftop. LGBTQ+ friendly, family-welcoming, and always alive.
